Your 45 minutes

What to do

New to contrast therapy? Here's how we recommend getting the best from your slot:

  1. Start: 1–2 min cold plunge
  2. 10–15 min in the sauna
  3. 1–2 min cold plunge
  4. 10–15 min in the sauna
  5. Finish: 1–2 min cold plunge

Always listen to your body, stay hydrated, and consult your GP or practitioner on whether sauna use is suitable for you.

FAQs

What do I need to bring with me?

Towel for drying, towel for the sauna, swimwear, water bottle, flip flops/sliders.

How long are the slots?

Each slot is 45 minutes.

Do I need to book in advance?

You can book online, or you can visit us onsite and we can find a slot for you.

What age do I have to be?

The minimum age to use our facilities is 18. We will not allow entry to U18s or those perceived to be. We may request ID.
