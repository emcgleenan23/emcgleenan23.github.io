What an event looks like

Your crowd, our heat

Every collaboration is different, but the shape is the same: we bring the wood-fired sauna and cold plunges to your venue (or host you at ours), run the sessions, and look after the heat — you bring the people. Works brilliantly for gyms and sports clubs, community groups, brand activations and wellness days.

Sessions can run as drop-in rotations for big groups or scheduled slots for smaller ones.
