Our story

Löyly — the steam that rises when water hits hot stone

Built by hand on the Tullysaran Road, ten minutes from Armagh.

We built this place by hand — the sauna, the plunges, the benches — on the Tullysaran Road, ten minutes from Armagh. No plastic wellness, no rush. Just a proper wood-fired stove, real steam, cold water, and the kind of conversation that only happens on a sauna bench.
