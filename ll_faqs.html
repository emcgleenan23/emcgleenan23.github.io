Before you book

FAQs

Everything to know before your first visit — what to bring, how hot, how cold, and the rest.

What do I need to bring with me?

Towel for drying, towel for the sauna, swimwear, water bottle, flip flops/sliders.

How long are the slots?

Each slot is 45 minutes.

When should I arrive?

You should aim to arrive just before your booking.

How many people are in each slot?

For our social sauna we limit the numbers to 8 people — this ensures everyone can have space and time in the sauna and cold plunge.

Do I need to book in advance?

You can book online, or you can visit us onsite and we can find a slot for you.

Are there changing rooms?

Yes, we have changing rooms available for all customers.

Is there parking on site?

We have parking available, but if possible please share a car to keep some spaces free.

How hot is the sauna?

We aim to keep the sauna between 80–90°C.

How cold is the cold plunge?

The cold plunge is kept at ambient temperature — cold enough to refresh you and give contrast after the sauna.

What age do I have to be?

The minimum age to use our facilities is 18. We will not allow entry to U18s or those perceived to be. We may request ID.

Is it safe if I have a medical condition?

If you have any medical conditions that may be adversely affected by the use of saunas, cold plunges, or extreme temperatures, please seek advice from a medical professional before using our facilities.

Can you bring the sauna to us?

Yes — our mobile sauna is available for private and commercial hire: events, retreats, sports clubs and swim spots. See Sauna Hire or email info@loyly.live with your date and location.
